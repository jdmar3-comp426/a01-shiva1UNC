Guide to Tolkien's The Silmarillion

About the book:

The Silmarillion is a collection of stories by author and scholar J.R.R. Tolkien that were published posthumously by his son Christopher Tolkien. The work, which is set in the same literary universe as The Lord of The Rings, goes into great detail on the history of the First Age of Middle Earth. The book is divided into five major sections:

  1. Ainulindalë
  2. Valaquenta
  3. Quenta Silmarillion
  4. Akallabêth
  5. Of the Rings of Power and the Third Age
